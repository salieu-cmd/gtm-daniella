05 · How the Funnel Works

Every P2 enrolment begins with a P1 purchase.

Path A
Direct booking

After buying P1, the buyer is shown a CTA to book a 1-to-1 call with Daniella directly. This is the primary route to P2 – the most critical piece of copy in the funnel.

Path B
Webinar route

A portion of P1 buyers are directed to a free educational webinar. The webinar ends with a CTA to book a call. Deliberately leaky in Phase 2 – optimised in Phase 3.

DM Closes
Warm conversions

Warm leads that convert through direct message. Low dependency by design – the funnel does the heavy lifting. DMs are a supplementary catch-net.

The signal that matters

A P1 buyer who gets a result will ask what comes next. That question is the conversion event. The post-purchase CTA is what captures it.
